INDUSTRIAL AGE 3.0

Entering the Industrial Age 3.0"" is an artwork that portrays the transition from traditional manufacturing to modern industry, marked by the adoption of digital technologies, automation, and artificial intelligence. The artwork depicts a factory floor with workers and machines, but it also features machine and other advanced equipment that demonstrate the changing nature of industrial production. The color scheme is predominantly gray and silver, which creates a sense of technological modernity and industrial efficiency. The lighting is also carefully designed to highlight the machines and the people working on the floor, emphasizing the interplay between human and machine in the modern industrial landscape."

LIVE WITH NATURE

This artwork explores the question of how humans should treat nature. It advocates for coexistence with nature, emphasizing that plants are an inherent part of the Earth's ecosystem, and should be respected not solely for their importance to humans, but for their intrinsic value as beings on this planet.
